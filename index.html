Mangino is a barangay in the city of Gapan, in the province of Nueva Ecija. Its population as determined by the 2020 Census was 12,922. This represented 10.51% of the total population of Gapan.

The origin of the name "Gapan City" is believed to come from the native word "Gapang", which was then shortened to "Gapan" by the Spanish colonizers. Some accounts suggest "Gapang" referred to plants that were abundant in the area. The town was later transferred to Nueva Ecija province in 1848, along with other settlements.
